flour and milk and sugar then bake

DECRIPTION

Cake? Oh, now that’s somethin’ different. It sits there, all pretty-like, layers stacked up like it’s hidin’ somethin’ inside. Soft, but not too soft—like it’s got just enough strength to hold its secrets. You cut into it, and it don’t scream, but somethin’ about the way it crumbles makes you wonder if it wanted to stay whole. And that sweetness? It ain’t natural. It’s the kinda sweet that masks things—hides the truth underneath all that sugar ‘til you don’t know if you’re tastin’ joy or somethin’ meant to keep you quiet

INGRIDIENTS

  • flour
  • sugar
  • milk

steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake pan.
  2. Gather all ingredients, including flour, sugar, butter, eggs, baking powder, salt, vanilla extract, and milk.
  3. In a mixing bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with milk, and mix until smooth.
  7. Stir in the vanilla extract for added flavor.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan, spreading it evenly.
  9. Bake for 25–35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  10. Remove from the oven and let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
  11. Transfer the cake to a wire rack to cool completely before frosting.
  12. Frost and decorate as desired, then serve and enjoy!
